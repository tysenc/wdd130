We offer amazing guided rafting options! New to rafting and just want to enjoy the ride and learn? Our experienced staff are ready from open to close, ready to help you through your experience and offer any questions you may have along the way! Make your way through rocky lanscape, forest, and sandy beach while having a blast! Top the trip off with a fancy stay at our top notch housing so the fun can last even longer.

We recognize the families with much knowledge and skill! Want to go on a ride with your your family? No worries! With routes perfect for group rafting, you can get experience like no other! Raft the rapids with friends or family on this day long experience. Ideal for large groups and offers great deals for many people. We offer rafts with room for as many as 14 people and room for storage! Come along for the action packed adventure, getting splashed with those around you!

Looking for a solo experience? Look no further, we have many options at White Water Rafting! Located in Idaho, we offer rentals or a bring-your-own experience. Get a taste of the forest rapids in this lovely, forested area that has much to show. With smaller groups, we have convenient and affordable housing options. There are also shallow spots for some lake fun for both casual rafting/floating or even swimming with extreme safety!
