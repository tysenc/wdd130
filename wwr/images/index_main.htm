Half-Day Whitewater Rafting Trips Near Glacier National Park

Raft alongside breathtaking views along the southern boundary of Glacier National Park

Half-Day Rafting Trips

Our half-day whitewater rafting trips on the Wild and Scenic Middle Fork of the Flathead River offer an exhilarating stretch of river that runs through the John F. Stevens Canyon bordering Glacier National Park and featuring numerous class II-III splashy rapids for the whole family.

River Miles

8 miles

Age Limits

Ages 5 & up. Age is dependent upon weather & river conditions. 50 lb. minimum

Trip Duration

Depending on the river level. 2-2 1/2 hours in May – Mid June to 4 hours by late August- early September.

River Rating & Rapids

Class II-IV depending on river flows. (Class III-IV in late May to early July; to moderate Class II-III later in the summer)

Rapids Include

Tunnel, Bonecrusher, Toilet Bowl, The Big Squeeze, Screaming Right-Hand Turn, Jaws, The Narrows, CBT and Pumphouse.

Rafting Times

May 1st – June 13th: 10:15, 2:15
June 14th – August 19th: 10:15, 11:15, 2:15, 3:15
August 20th – Sept 4th: 10:15, 2:15, 3:15
Sept 5th – Sept 20th: 10:15, 2:15
(Resort check-in time 30 mins prior to trip time)

Rates

Adult: $80.00

Child (12 and under): $60.00

Private Boat: $640.00 (Limit 8 people)

Private Trip: $800 per boat (Limit 8 people)

Group rates available for 10+, 20+ and 30+

Half-Day Scenic Float

Highlight your vacation experience with a scenic rafting trip by venturing into a quiet, natural world full of wildlife, such as deer, elk, beaver, herons, ducks, geese, and raptors like osprey and bald eagles.

We know that whitewater rafting trips aren’t for everyone. Our scenic river tour gives you the opportunity to fully immerse yourself in the spectacular beauty of Glacier National Park. You can sit back and relax on this peaceful stretch of the Wild and Scenic Middle Fork of the Flathead River. Our friendly, interpretive river guides will do all the work to safely navigate you down the river. Perfect for families with young children, seniors, and anyone who just wants to relax.

Half-Day Thrillseeker Sport Raft

This rafting trip is for the daring paddler that wants a smaller boat and crew to make for a wilder whitewater rafting experience! Our 13’ sport rafts are the perfect option for the Middle Fork of the Flathead River. 

This trip starts with a short ride to Mocassin Creek, eight miles upstream of West Glacier. From there, your guide will instruct you on river safety and proper paddling technique as you paddle your way to the Middle Fork of the Flathead River. Once you get to John Stevens Canyon, the river picks up speed and your adventure really begins. Practice your skills navigating the rapids from the canyon to the historic Old Bridge, then don’t forget to check out the photos of your run through Bonecrusher when you return to our main Chalet!

Half-Day 1 & 2 Person Funyak

This is one of the most exciting trips on the river, and designed for the more adventurous paddler! Brave the whitewater rapids in a single or double inflatable kayak. The waves are much bigger when you are sitting at water level!

This trip starts eight miles upstream of West Glacier, at Mocassin Creek. From there, you’ll paddle your way to the Middle Fork of the Flathead River. Practice your skills navigating the rapids from John Stevens Canyon to the historic Old Bridge, then don’t forget to check out the photos of your run through the Bonecrusher rapid when you return to our main Chalet!
